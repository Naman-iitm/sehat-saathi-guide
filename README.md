Imagine having a friendly health guide in your pocket, speaking your language, helping you understand symptoms, find nearby hospitals, and access government health schemes. That's Sehat Saathi!

Sehat Saathi means "Health Companion" in Hindi - and that's exactly what we aim to be. A simple, helpful friend who makes healthcare less confusing and more accessible.

â“ Common Questions
Q: Is this a replacement for doctors?
A: No! Sehat Saathi is a guide, not a doctor. Always consult real healthcare professionals for medical advice.

Q: Is my data private?
A: Yes! We don't store your personal health information. Everything stays on your device.

Q: Does it work offline?
A: Partially! You can access basic information, but maps and AI chat need internet.

Q: Can I use it on my phone?
A: Absolutely! It's designed to work perfectly on mobile phones.
